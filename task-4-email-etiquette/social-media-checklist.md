# Social Media Do's ✅ and Don'ts ❌

### The Do's ✅
1. **Do check your privacy settings:** Regularly review who can see your posts and personal information to ensure you aren't oversharing with the public.
2. **Do think before you post:** Ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future employer, mentor, or your family seeing the content.
3. **Do build a positive digital footprint:** Share articles, projects, and achievements that reflect your professional interests and personal growth.
4. **Do give proper credit:** If you share someone else's artwork, quote, or research, always tag them or cite the original source.
5. **Do report harassment:** Use platform tools to block and report cyberbullying, hate speech, or inappropriate messages.

### The Don'ts ❌
1. **Don't overshare personal details:** Never post your exact home address, phone number, schedule, or financial information publicly.
2. **Don't post in the heat of the moment:** Avoid reacting emotionally or angrily to posts or comments; always take time to cool down before responding to prevent saying something you might regret.
3. **Don't engage in internet arguments:** Avoid getting dragged into "flame wars" or toxic comment sections; it rarely ends well and leaves a permanent public record.
4. **Don't post illegal or inappropriate activities:** Pictures or videos involving illicit or dangerous activities can lead to serious real-world consequences or job offer withdrawals.
5. **Don't spread unverified information:** Avoid sharing sensational news or rumors without fact-checking the source first to prevent the spread of misinformation.
